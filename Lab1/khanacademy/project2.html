Ostri (Georgian Spicy Beef Stew)

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Beef (chuck or stew meat) 1 kg
Tomatoes (fresh or canned) 3 large
Onion 1 large
Garlic cloves 4 cloves
Red chili peppers 2 (adjust to taste)
Coriander (fresh) 1 bunch
Water or beef broth 2 cups
Vegetable oil 3 tbsp
Salt & Pepper to taste

Step 1: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.

Step 2: Add beef cubes, brown them on all sides.

Step 3: Add chopped tomatoes, garlic, and chili peppers. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 4: Pour in water or broth, add salt and pepper, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for about 1.5 hours until beef is tender.

Step 5: Add chopped fresh coriander just before serving. Mix well and enjoy!

Khinkal (Georgian Dumplings)

  • Time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: 4-6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 3 cups
Water 1 cup (warm)
Egg 1 large
Salt 1 tsp
Beef or lamb (for broth) 500 g
Onion 1 large
Garlic 3 cloves
Vegetable oil or butter 2 tbsp
Fresh herbs (cilantro, parsley) 1 bunch
Salt & Pepper to taste

Step 1: Mix flour, warm water, egg, and salt to make a dough. Knead until smooth, then let rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Roll out dough and cut into small squares or triangles.

Step 3: Boil beef or lamb with onions and garlic to make a rich broth.

Step 4: Cook dumplings in boiling salted water until they float to the surface (about 5-7 minutes). Drain.

Step 5: Serve dumplings with broth, garnish with sautéed onions and fresh herbs.
